# Data Privacy

**General**<br>
Based on Article 13 of the Swiss Federal Constitution and the data protection provisions of the Swiss Confederation (Data Protection Act, nFADP), every person has the right to protection of their privacy and to protection against misuse of their personal data.

**Data Collection**<br>
This website is hosted via GitHub Pages. We do not use cookies, trackers, or analytics tools on this landing page. However, the hosting provider (GitHub Inc.) may collect server log files (IP address, browser type) for security purposes.
